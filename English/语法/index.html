# 第一章 并列句

# 并列举的构成

# 表示顺接的并列连词

# 表示转折的并列连词

  • ... but ... “... 但是 ...”
  • ... yet ...

遇到转折后，要重点看转折后的句子，往往考点在转折之后的句子

eg:

  1. he environment is obviously important, but its role has =remained= obscure
  2. We all understand that at some level, yet as medical consumers we treat death as a problem to be solved.

# 表示选择的并列连词

  • ... or ... "... 或者..."
  • either.. or... (二选一)
  • neither.. nor... "既不... 也不..."(两者都不选)

eg:

  1. Either Entergy never really intended to live by those commitments, or it simply didn't foresee what would happen next.

# 表示因果的并列连词

  1. ...for... "... 因为..."
  2. ...so... "... 所以..."

eg:

  1. Reporter tend to be part of a broadly defined social and cultural elite, so they work tends to reflect the conventional values of this elite.

# 补充

  • 并列来纳茨不一定非要连接句子，也可以连接词或词组

  • 并列句不仅可以并列两届是，还可以并列更多事件，依次加入合适的并列连词即可

  • 如果多个并列连词相同，保留最后一个，前面替换为逗号

  • 如果多个并列连词不同，则不能替换。

# 并列句的省略

并列句中如果有多个相同的成分，可以省略

eg:

  1. The program keeps track of your progress and provides detailed feedback on your performance and improvement.
  2. ... the peoples .. died out or became assimilated and lost their native languages.
  3. But most women today are coping with a lot of obligations ... and feeling the strain.
  4. ... the federal government must support job training programs, raise the minimum wage, and fund more low-cost housing.
  5. Humility requires you to recognize weakness in your own arguments and sometimes also to accept reasons on the opposite side

# 内容小结

  1. 并列句的构成： 多个简单句 + 并联连词（四类并列连词是重点）
  2. 并列句的省略：相同的成分可以省略，余下的成分保持不变

# 练习

  1. We neither understand nor respect each other ...
  2. They cross-check sources and prefer news from different perspectives ...
  3. ... the editor would accept the paper for publication or decline it.
  4. Formerly it lasted three days, but by the 1980s it more commonly lasted a day and a half.
  5. In Europe, taxes account for up to four-fifths of the retail price, so even quite big changes in the price of crude have a more muted effect on pump prices than in the past.

# 考场攻略

  1. 并列句的四类并列连词建议重点掌握

# 第二章 复合句

复合句 = 主句 + 从句

America's new plan to buy up toxic assets will not work unless banks mark assets to levels which buyers find attractive.

# 第一节 名词性从句

# 一、宾语从句

# (一) 宾语从句含义

一个句子作宾语，放在另一个主句中作宾语。

Justice Anthony Kennedy wrote that the previous decisions were flawed.

# (二) 宾语从句的写法

  1. 陈述句变宾语从句

    陈述句什么都不用变，直接在陈述句前面加 that

  2. 特殊疑问句变宾语从句

    调整语序，将特殊疑问句变成陈述句，即将主语提前

  3. 一般疑问句变宾语从句

    将一般疑问句变成陈述句，然后用 if/whether 放在从句的句首

    I don't know if/whether other clients are going to abandon me, too ...

Why do so many Americans distrust what they read in their newspapers?

不管主句是什么语句，从句都是陈述语句。

  • Dr. Worm acknowledges that these figures are conservative.
  • We suddenly can't remember where we put the keys just a moment ago ...
  • I don't know if other clients are going to abandon me, too ...

主句 + 连接词 + 陈述句

# 宾语从句的位置

This may also explain why we are not usually sensitive to our own smells.. 动宾

... users could tell advertisers that they did not want to be followed. 动宾： 及物动词 + 人 + 从句

This and other similar cases raise the question of whether there is still a line between the court and politics.

... he felt certain that he never could have succeeded with mathematics. 形容词 + 宾语从句 -- 形容词 +(介词)+ 宾语从句

You might even be tempted to assume that humanity has little future to look forward to.

Neither of these patterns is borne out by the analysis, suggesting thart the structures of the languages are lineage-specific and not governed by universals.

# 二、表语从句

# (一) 表语从句含义

一个句子作表语，放在另一个主句中作表语。

# (二) 表语从句的写法

主句 + 连接词 + 从句

  • Part of the issue is that airports have only so much room for screening lanes (安检通道).

  • This is where developing new habits comes in.

  • This is why repeated attempts at reform legislation have failed in recent years...

# (三) 表语从句的位置

表语从句位于系动词的后面

  • For Williams, these activities become what he calls "electronic heroin"
  • Yet the fact remains that the <u>merger movement</u>(合并的运动) must be watched.
  • Human nature being what it is, most people <u>stick with</u>(坚持) default settings.
  • The explanation for insensitivity to smell seems to be that the brain finds it inefficient (低效率的) to keep all smell receptors (接收器) working all the time but can create new receptors if necessary

# 三、主语从句

# (一) 主语从句含义

一个句子作主语，放在另一个主句中作主语。

<u>What is being called artificial general intelligence ...</u> continues to evade scientists.

# (二) 主语从句的写法

连接词 + 从句 + 主句

  • <u>That the seas are being overfished</u> has been known for years.
  • What motivated (所激励...) him .. was his zeal (热情的) for "fundamental fairness"
  • And whether the community's work contributes much to an overall accumulation of knowledge is doubtful.
# (三) 主语从句的位置

主语从句位于主句的句首，充当主语

或写成 it 做形式主语

  • Just how people think is still far too complex to be understood ...

  • It did not matter what was done in the experiment ...

  • It is becoming lee clear, however, that such a theory would be a simplification...

# (四) 主语从句的句型模板

It is done + 主语从句（表达人们对一件事的观点看法）

  • This year, it was proposed that the system be changed ...
  • It was argued at the end of the 19th century that humans do not cry because they are sad ...

It is + adj./n. + 主语从句（表达对一件事的评价）

  • It is not yet clear how much more effective airline security has become ..
  • Second, it is surely a good thing that the money and attention come to science rather than go elsewhere.

# 四、同位语从句

# (一) 同位语从句的含义

一个句子做同位语，放到大的主句当中，用于解释说明抽象名词，放在抽象名词的后面

  • So does the idea that decisions made by AI systems should be explainable, transparent, and fair
# (二) 同位语从句的写法

抽象n. + 连接词 + 陈述句

  • For my pat, I have no idea whether GiIbert is a great conductor or even a good one
# (三) 同位语从句的位置

抽象名词后

idea, opinion, view, fact, evidence, question, doubt, reason, demand, theory, belief, possibility, chance, hope, contention, guarantee ...

  • In other words, they all share a view that the international tax system has failed to keep up with the current economy
  • A century ago, Freud formulated his revolutionary theory that dreams were the disguised shadows of our unconscious desires and fears ...

# 补充

一句中有多个名词性从句

What researchers such as Ransom Myers and Boris Worm have shown is just how fast things are changing.

# 内容小结

写法基本相同，位置不同

  • 名词性从句
    • 宾语从句 三种位置
      • 动宾
        • 动单宾：及物动词 + 从句
        • 动双宾：及物动词 + 人 + 从句
      • 介宾
      • 形容词 + 宾语从句
    • 表语从句 一种位置
      • 系动词后（常用的是 be 动词后）
    • 主句从句
      • 句首
      • 句尾（句首用形式主语 it)
    • 同位语从句 一种位置
      • 抽象名词后
